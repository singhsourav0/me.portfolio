I am Sourav Kumar.

A Computer Science Engineering graduate (2025) with more than two years of hands-on experience in the data domain, working across data analytics, applied statistics, machine learning, deep learning, and Generative AI. My foundation lies in statistical analysis and exploratory data analysis (EDA), including hypothesis testing, regression, and data validation, which I apply to understand and prepare data for downstream modeling.

I have practical experience of building machine learning models using a range of classical and neural network–based algorithms, with a focus on applying the right techniques. My deep learning work spans convolutional neural networks (CNN) for computer vision, recurrent and transformer-based architectures for NLP, and end-to-end predictive modeling for real-world applications.

Beyond modeling, have exposure of data engineering and system design aspects, including data ingestion, cleaning, feature engineering, and pipeline automation. I have experience designing scalable data workflows, integrating multiple data sources, and preparing data for production-grade machine learning systems.

I am actively involved in MLOps practices, including experiment tracking, versioning of data and models, CI/CD pipelines, containerization using Docker, and deploying models as APIs or applications. I have worked with cloud platforms to deploy, monitor, and maintain machine learning systems in production environments.

More recently, my focus has expanded to applied Generative AI, where I build LLM-powered applications using RAG pipelines, prompt engineering, and vector-based retrieval systems. Across all my work, I prioritize building practical, scalable solutions that translate raw data into reliable, real-world intelligence.
